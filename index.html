B. K. BAGIA

Lights and Sound
since 1952








Established in 1952 by BhuraBhai KarshanBhai Bagia, B. K. Bagia Lights and Sound has been a pioneer in the lighting and sound industry in Junagadh, Gujarat. As the first firm to undertake wiring work as contractors in Junagadh, our legacy of excellence spans over seven decades. Initially run by BhuraBhai KarshanBhai Bagia, whose work was highly commendable, the firm was subsequently managed by the late DayaBhai BhuraBhai Bagia and late VinodBhai BhuraBhai Bagia. Today, the tradition of quality and dedication continues under the leadership of PromodBhai BhuraBhai Bagia and Akash VinodBhai Bagia. As licensed government electrical contractors, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch services with a steadfast commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

What We Offer

At B. K. Bagia Lights and Sound, we take pride in offering a comprehensive range of services to meet all your lighting and sound needs. As a government-licensed contractor, we specialize in handling official contracts for lights and sound, ensuring the highest standards of quality and compliance.

Our Comprehensive Services

Government Contracts

As licensed government electrical contractors, we undertake various government contracts for lighting and sound projects. Our team of skilled professionals is equipped to handle large-scale projects with precision and efficiency, ensuring that all work meets the stringent requirements set by government regulations.

Event Lighting and Decoration

We provide complete light decoration services for a wide array of events, including weddings, engagements, and other special occasions. Our team is dedicated to transforming your venue into a beautifully lit space that enhances the ambiance and creates memorable experiences for you and your guests.

Rental Services

In addition to our contracting and decoration services, we offer a range of rental options to suit your needs:

  • Coolers and Fans: Keep your event cool and comfortable with our high-quality coolers and fans.

  • Generators: Ensure uninterrupted power supply with our reliable generators, perfect for outdoor and large-scale events.

  • Lighting Materials: We provide various lighting materials including par lights, halogen lights, sharpin lights, lighting chandelier and led series lights, to enhance your event's decor.

  • Sound Systems: Our top-notch sound systems ensure crystal-clear audio for your event. We offer a variety of speakers, microphones and other audio equipments like audio mixers, amplifiers etc for rent, suitable for speeches, performances, and more.
